Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025

Ball Knowledge ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Ball Knowledge web application and mobile app (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use the Service, and the choices you have regarding that information.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use the Service, we may collect:

  • Email address and password (for email/password accounts)
  • Username you choose to provide
  • Favorite teams you select during setup (one per league)
  • Content you create in multiplayer games (player names entered in Draft With Friends, Custom Mystery Player challenges)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device identifiers, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and general location (country/region level)
  • Usage data: screens viewed, features used, time spent
  • Gameplay data: daily game submissions, scores, streak history, XP earned, leaderboard rankings
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

1.3 Information From Third Parties

If you sign in using Google Sign In, we receive a unique identifier, your email address, and your name from Google. We do not receive your Google password.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • Personalize your experience based on your favorite teams and selected leagues
  • Track your game streaks, XP, level, and progress across sessions
  • Populate and maintain weekly and all-time leaderboards
  • Process and display Blind Rank 5 community vote distributions
  • Send push notifications about daily game resets and streak alerts (with your permission)
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent game submissions
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Advertising

Ball Knowledge uses Google AdSense and Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, subject to your device's advertising preferences and applicable regulations.

Ads are displayed only on screens with substantive content. Ads are never shown on authentication screens, onboarding screens, or loading states.

You can opt out of personalized advertising on iOS via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and on Android via Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization. You may also visit google.com/settings/ads to manage your Google ad preferences.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database hosting, authentication, and storage. Supabase processes data only on our behalf.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdSense / AdMob. Google's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics: We use PostHog for usage analytics. This service receives aggregated usage data to help us understand how the Service is used. For more information, see PostHog's Privacy Policy.
  • Crash Reporting: We use Sentry to monitor app stability and diagnose crashes. When a crash occurs, Sentry may collect crash logs, stack traces, device information (OS, app version), and screenshots of the screen at the time of the crash. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. For more information, see Sentry's Privacy Policy.
  • Public Leaderboard Data: Your username, level, XP totals, and streak are visible to other users on the leaderboard. You can view other users' public profile data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: If Ball Knowledge is involved in a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and gameplay data for as long as your account is active. Weekly XP is reset to zero each Monday as part of normal Service operation — this reset affects the weekly leaderboard only; your lifetime XP and level are never reset. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including HTTPS encryption for all transmitted data, secure token storage on your device (iOS Keychain, Android EncryptedSharedPreferences), row-level security on all database tables, and short-lived access tokens with rotating refresh tokens.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Ball Knowledge is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us at ballknowingdevelopers@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly. The Service requires users to confirm they are at least 13 years old at registration.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to opt out of personalized advertising. To exercise these rights, contact us at ballknowingdevelopers@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents have additional rights under CCPA, including the right to know what information we collect and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. International Users

Ball Knowledge is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by updating the Effective Date above and, where appropriate, by push notification or email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
