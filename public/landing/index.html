Daily Games

Six games. Four leagues. Every day.

Six daily games reset every night at midnight ET across the NFL, NBA, MLB, and NHL. Build a streak by coming back every day. Your progress, XP, and streaks are saved to your account.

Mystery Player

One hidden player per league per day. Guess by typing any player name and get color-coded feedback on their attributes — team, position, age, height, college, draft round, years pro. Green is exact. Yellow is close with a directional arrow. Gray is wrong. Up to 8 guesses to identify the player through pure deduction.

Max 8 guesses · Best score for a 1-guess solve

Blind Showdown

Two players side by side with only their stats visible — no names, no teams. Pick the statistically superior player before the identity reveal. Matchups are curated to challenge assumptions: a lesser-known player outproducing a star, or a former award winner up against a rising challenger.

New matchup every day per league

Blind Rank 5

Five players from the same league revealed one at a time. Place each player in one of five ranked slots before the next reveal. Once placed, that slot is locked — no rearranging. After all five, see how the entire community ranked the same set.

Community results revealed after enough submissions

Sports Trivia

Three questions per league in ascending difficulty: Easy, Medium, and Ball Knowledge. The Ball Knowledge tier separates fans who watch games from fans who study the sport. A timer runs from your first question to your last answer. Personal bests tracked per league.

3 questions · Timer tracks your best time

Power Play

A fast-money style daily challenge. Answer rapid-fire questions and hit the target score before you run out of chances. Every question is different — this one rewards both speed and accuracy.

Target score to beat · New questions daily

Auto Complete

Fill in the sports search results. You're shown the beginning of a sports-related search query and have to predict how real fans actually complete it. Tests pop culture sports knowledge as much as stats knowledge.

New prompts daily

Multiplayer Games

Seven games to play with friends.

Seven multiplayer party games playable on a single device. Pass the phone around the group — no extra accounts, no separate devices, no internet required during gameplay. Perfect for watch parties or any group that thinks they know sports better than everyone else in the room.

Sports Imposter

Social deduction game. Most players get a real athlete's full career profile. One or two impostors get nothing and must bluff through discussion. Vote out who you think is faking. Difficulty settings from casual to Ball Knowledge tier.

Sports Wavelength

A spectrum guessing game. One player gives a sports reference as a clue pointing to a hidden position on a spectrum — like "Clutch ↔ Choke Artist" or "Role Player ↔ Franchise Player." Everyone else places their needle. Points for proximity to the target.

Draft With Friends

A snake draft with a random category: "Best Lakers of All Time," "Athletes You'd Want in a Bar Fight," "Most Entertaining Players to Watch." Take turns picking. The board fills up, you share it, and the group argues about who won.

Who Am I?

A sports guessing game where each player has an athlete attached to their forehead (visible to everyone except them). Ask yes/no questions to figure out who you are before anyone else does.

13 Words or Less

Describe a player, team, or sports moment using 13 words or fewer. Fast, creative, and harder than it sounds — especially when the category is a niche stat line or a game that happened 20 years ago.

Custom Mystery Player

Create a custom mystery player challenge and share it with friends via a code. They get the same guessing experience as the daily game — but you chose the player.

Coverage

All four major North American sports leagues.

NFL

National Football League — 32 teams. All 6 daily games available for NFL. Mystery Player attributes include position, college, draft round, height, and years pro. Select your favorite NFL team to personalize your home screen experience.

NBA

National Basketball Association — 30 teams. The stat-heavy nature of basketball makes Blind Showdown especially interesting — PPG, APG, RPG, and shooting percentages all visible before the reveal. College and draft information adds depth to the Mystery Player game.

MLB

Major League Baseball — 30 teams. Mystery Player attributes include batting hand, throwing arm, and college vs. international background — details that separate fans who grew up with the game from casual viewers. The deepest statistical sport in the game.

NHL

National Hockey League — 32 teams. Nationality attributes and position groupings (Forward, Defense, Goalie) make NHL games uniquely different from the other three leagues. The international depth of hockey creates some of the hardest Mystery Player puzzles in the app.
